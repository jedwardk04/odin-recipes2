Ramen

Description

Boiled ramen noodles in a broth flavored to your choosing, spiced to your level of desire

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth Flavoring
  • Hard Boiled Egg
  • Cooked Beef

Steps

  1. Boil your water
  2. Add your noodles
  3. Add your broth flavoring
  4. Add your hard boiled egg cut in half for aestethics
  5. Add your cooked beef
