Chicken Tacos

Description

Tacos using shredded as the main source of meat, with any of the normal taco fixings used to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Diced Onion
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Place shredded chicken on tortilla of your choosing
  2. Place shredded cheese onto taco and microwave 20 seconds to get gooey melted cheese
  3. Add your tomatoes and onion
  4. Add nice big scoops of your sour cream
  5. Add as much salsa as you want and enjoy!
